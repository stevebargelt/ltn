Our Mission

Labor Tubs Northwest delivers birthing tubs to hospitals, birth centers and homes throughout the Greater Seattle Area. Our comprehensive service includes on-call 24-hour delivery, set up, and phone support, as well as tub removal. We also offer self-service options for clients planning a home birth.
